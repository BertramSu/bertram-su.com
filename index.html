About me

Bertram Su is a junior at Case Western Reserve University where he is majoring in computer science with a minor in economics.

Having been interested in computer science since high school, his recent addition of an econ minor developed after being fascinated with the impact of different monetary policies. After helping data structures concepts "click" with friends, Bertram decided to become a supplemental intructor for data structures and algorithms the spring of his sophomore year, creating worksheets and holding collaborative sessions for further understanding of the class material.

Bertram aspires to pursue a career in software development and eventually lead a team of developers. In his free time, he enjoys lifting weights, playing tennis, sampling shows, and playing various Nintendo games.
